Places I visited

Unity Park

Unity Park was established in 2019. The park is located at the Grand Palace in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, a compound that has been a seat of state for more than a century and is finally open to the public.

Friendship Square

I enjoyed walking around the park; it's so green and full of beautiful flowers. I admire the thought of planting the metal gym equipment for simple workouts; it's really nice for the young generations finding a place to hang out and work out at the same time.

Debre Libanos Monastery

Debre Libanos is an Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo monastery, lying northwest of Addis Ababa in the North Shewa Zone of the Oromia Region.
